Guide to the Galaxy

Andromeda Galaxy

Description

The Andromeda Galaxy (Messier 31, NGC 224) is a spiral galaxy located about 2.54 million light-years away from Earth, in the constellation of Andromeda.

Interesting Data

  1. Besides the Milky Way, it is the most studied galaxy
  2. Its two satellite galaxies, Messier 32 and Messier 110, are visible in binoculars.
  3. Its distance from Earth is still unclear.
