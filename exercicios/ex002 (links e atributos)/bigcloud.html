Large Magellanic Cloud

Description

Large Magellanic Cloud (commonly abbreviated LMC) is a dwarf galaxy satellite that orbits around the Milky Way.

Interesting Data

  1. It is rich in gases and dust
  2. It was baptized by Fernão de Magalhães
  3. The Large Magellanic Cloud is one of the closest galaxies to the Milky Way

Others Galaxys: Andromeda, Black Eyes.
